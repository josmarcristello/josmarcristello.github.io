The VMFM - Vibration Mass Flow Meter

The host pipeline is excited with a piezoelectric transducer, and the mechanical vibrations are read with two vibration sensors. Directly measures mass flow rate and fluid density.

faq

Got questions? We’ve got answers. If you have some other questions, feel free to send us an email to support@InFlow.com

What is VMFM? How it works?

It is a unique and innovative mass flow meter. You simply install it in your pipeline, connect it with our own provided software and you're done! We can always provide support if you stumble into any problems.

How does it integrate into my network?

We provide ready SCADA code, and typically, connect by cables. We also have a wireless version coming up in BETA version that we can offer for customers that are bringing their entire plant into the cloud.

Is there a maximum temperature I should worry about?

For now, we are using the VMFM for up to 300°C, because that has been what our customers have needed at most. If you have a more stringent process, please let us know. We are excited for more challenges!

Corrosion is really critical for my plant. Do you offer other materials?

For corrosion sensitive pipelines, we offer different metallic alloys for the casing. Please talk to one of our specialists.
